DESTINATION WEDDING DECOR

Whether you plan to have your destination wedding near the ocean, the mountains or the ski slopes, MadeInHeaven helps in enhancing the gorgeous setting of your wedding venue with impeccable wedding décor ideas. With our unique décor designs and concepts, no matter where your wedding will take place, one of our decor themes is sure to suit yourstyle! From Goa to Jodhpur, take a look at these breathtaking wedding destinations that will sway you to indulge in your wedding wanderlust.



GOA

'Sandy toes and salty kisses'- This is one line that perfectly describes a wedding in the party destination of India. From a fun whimsical mehendi to a serene white wedding, browse through some of our gorgeous decor setups at the luxurious Grand Hyatt Goa and The LaLit Golf and Spa Resort.






















JAIPUR

Looking to celebrate your special day at a heritage castle or an ancient haveli or fort? Jaipur is your perfect choice! The capital of Rajasthan, that was once home to the royal families, is also known as ‘The Pink city’ for its trademark building color. It houses some of the most beautiful palatial properties such as Fairmont, Jaipur, Rambagh Palace and Jai Mahal Palace.























KARJAT

We executed a beautiful farmhouse wedding in Karjat that was an intimate yet lavish affair. From a pop-art themed Mehendi, a Bollywood themed Sangeet to a traditional Indian wedding ceremony, this wedding was surely an unforgettable experience for all!






















MUMBAI

The fashion capital of India has always been a favorite wedding destination choice among couples! From a wide array of properties, caterers, artist and entertainment options, Mumbai never fails to please! Check out some of our stylish Mumbai wedding setups below.






















UDAIPUR

Elegant palaces, alluring royalty, and rich culture make Udaipur a top favorite for couples looking for a grand wedding. With its resplendent regal palaces and romantic gardens, this majestic city is the perfect locale for a union which is both magical and rooted in Indian traditions. Take a look at some of our work at the scenic Jagmandir, Radisson Blu Udaipur Palace Resort & Spa and Zenana Mahal.






















BAHRAIN

With tonnes of venue options to choose from that cater to all your needs, Bahrain has become an upcoming choice for couples looking for an international wedding destination. The latest wedding we designed in Hotel Sofitel Bahrain Zallaq Thalassa Sea & Spa had a kitschy themed mehendi, a glamorous Sangeet, a stunning wedding by the sea and a traditional Bahrain souk to end the splendid wedding festivities. See some of the photos below.






















JODHPUR

This popular tourist destination is the perfect choice for couples looking for a grand elaborate wedding. Umaid Bhawan Palace is one of the largest royal residences in the world, this golden-yellow sandstone showcases Jodhpur’s gallantry and romance like no other!
